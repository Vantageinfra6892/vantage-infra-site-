Chapter 02

Artisanal Lime Surfaces & Flooring

Mineral chemistry and carbonation dynamics engineered for high-end architecture.

While standard synthetic coatings look static and trap moisture, our authentic lime treatments interact with their environment. Through slow atmospheric carbonation, these finishes pull carbon dioxide from the air, returning to natural limestone over time.

Interactive Material Simulator

Select Finish Formulation

Layer 01

Structural Base (Thappi)

Coarse slaked lime combined with specific local aggregates. Provides the high mechanical grip needed for the masonry substrate.

Layer 02

Stress Equalization Layer (Lohi)

A fine-mesh intermediary layer that balances curing stresses and evens out moisture movement across the surface.

Layer 03

Carbonation Sheen Finish

The final burnished mineral layer. It creates an ultra-dense surface that draws in ambient CO₂ to lock in its final stone-like texture.

Premium Lime Floors

Seamless High-Traffic Mineral Floors

A durable, non-elastic lime floor finish. It offers a cool, stone-like texture underfoot with varied, natural tones that resist cracks through steady mineral carbonation.

Araish System

Traditional Shell/Marble Burnishing

Classic multi-layered lime finish burnished with polished stones to a glass-smooth gloss, reflecting soft interior light with natural depth.

Tadelakt

Hydrophobic Soap-Sealed Plaster

A dense Moroccan finish compacted and treated with olive oil soaps, creating a water-resistant, tactile barrier perfect for wet rooms and custom showers.

Premium Limewashing

Pure Mineral Pigment Washing

A breathable mineral wash that binds to masonry rather than sitting on top of it. It develops a soft, chalky appearance that won't peel or blister under high heat.
