The Lime Studio

Authentic, breathable mineral plasters, handcrafted Araish, and living lime flooring systems.

Modern synthetic paints and plastic-based micro-concrete sealers form suffocating barriers that trap moisture, leading to internal peeling, dampness, and static, artificial finishes. The Lime Studio treats walls as breathing organic surfaces.

Through the ancient science of slow atmospheric carbonation, our premium slaked lime treatments continuously draw in carbon dioxide from the surrounding air. This chemistry binds the material to its brick substrate, transforming raw plaster into solid, crystalline limestone over decades. The result is a series of seamless, light-diffusing textures that reject mold, regulate indoor air quality, and develop an elegant patina.
