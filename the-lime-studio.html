Section 01 // Surfaces

Lime Wash

Walls That Breathe. Spaces That Feel Alive.

Classic • Cloud • Roman • Wabi-Sabi • Textured • Stone • Mineral Washes

Every Lime Wash surface is handcrafted using aged lime and natural mineral pigments, creating depth, movement, and texture that conventional paints can never replicate.

Unlike synthetic coatings that simply cover a wall, Lime Wash becomes part of it. Each layer reacts naturally with light, creating subtle tonal variations that evolve throughout the day and continue to develop character over time.

Section 02 // Structural Floors

Lime Flooring

Floors Designed to Last Generations.

Araish • Oxide • Terrazzo • Lime Concrete • Exterior Systems

Before marble became a luxury, before vitrified tiles became the standard, some of history's most extraordinary buildings were finished with lime floors.

Today, Vantage Infra reimagines this timeless material through modern engineering, creating seamless flooring systems that remain naturally cool, exceptionally durable, and effortlessly elegant. Jointless by design. Comfortable underfoot. Crafted to age beautifully.
