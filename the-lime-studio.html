Section 01 // Surfaces

Lime Wash

Walls That Breathe. Spaces That Feel Alive.

Available Editorial Washes:

Classic Lime Wash • Cloud Wash • Roman Wash • Wabi-Sabi Wash • Textured Lime Wash • Stone Wash • Mineral Wash

Every Lime Wash surface is handcrafted using aged slaked lime and natural mineral pigments, creating an organic depth, tactile movement, and texture that conventional plastic paints can never replicate.

Unlike synthetic coatings that simply cover a wall, Lime Wash chemically petrifies straight into it. Each layer reacts beautifully with natural light, creating subtle tonal variations that evolve throughout the day and develop deep human character over time.

"One wall. Thousands of subtle variations. Light dances differently across every brushstroke, creating spaces that feel quieter, warmer, and more human."

Perfect For: Luxury Residences • Boutique Hotels • Restaurants • Resorts • Wellness Spaces • Heritage Restoration

Section 02 // Structural Floors

Lime Flooring

Floors Designed to Last Generations.

Available Flooring Systems:

Araish Flooring (Mirror-Polished Luxury) • Oxide Lime Flooring • Lime Terrazzo • Lime Concrete • Exterior Landscape Lime Flooring

Before marble became a luxury metric, and long before vitrified tiles became the mass standard, some of history's most extraordinary monumental buildings were finished with lime flooring systems.

Today, Vantage Infra reimagines this timeless material through modern material engineering, creating seamless flooring expanses that remain jointless by design, naturally cool underfoot, and completely free of artificial grout lines or interruptions.

Every Vantage lime floor is engineered as a comprehensive, layered system—not simply a decorative topping. Each level is optimized for compressive strength, thermal mass insulation, and long-term elasticity, growing structurally stronger as continuous carbonation seals the matrix over time.
